    The first player to reach a score of 100 wins the game.

    For each turn, the current player can roll or pass their turn to the other player.

    Rolling will increase the player's possible points by the die's amount. However, these possible points will be lost if the player rolls a 1 and will automatically pass their turn to the other player.

    Passing will save the round's possible points to the player's score and will pass the turn to the other player. Score points cannot be lost. If a player's score reaches 100 or above, that player wins the game.
